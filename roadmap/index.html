What's Next?

With v10.0.0, Improve-ImgSLI is already a workspace platform: pick a session, compare two images or several in a grid, and save projects. Looking ahead, the goal is a broader media-comparison toolkit — stronger project workflows and, eventually, side-by-side video comparison.

Planned Features & Improvements

  • Richer project workflows: Workspaces and the session picker are already in v10. Next up: undo and redo across sessions, more reliable multi-compare state, and smoother ways to reopen recent projects.
  • Video comparison: A dedicated tab for comparing videos is the natural next step after the current workspace foundation. That is the main long-term direction.
  • A deeper video editor: The timeline and export path keep getting better, but a fuller editing experience is still planned before video comparison can land cleanly.
